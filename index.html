    Products

    Cultivators, Suppliers, Exporters and Processors of Green Cardamom, Black Pepper, Clove, Nutmeg, Mace and Dry ginger.

    Cardamom

    Cardamom is native to the evergreen forests of India. It is one of the world’s very ancient spices and universally known as the “Queen of spices”. Green cardamom is one of the most expensive spices which need a few to impart aromatic complex flavour. Cardamom also consists of essential components such as melatonin that is pretty useful in increasing the metabolic rate.

    Black Pepper

    Black pepper, “The king of spices” is the world's most traded spice. Its spiciness is due to piperine a chemical compound, which is a different kind of spicy from the capsaicin characteristic of chili peppers. It act as an immunity booster by absorbing essential nutrients. It promotes gut health, offer pain relief, reduce cholesterol and blood sugar level and much more

    Clove

    Clove has been used for thousands of years in Asia not only as a spice but also as a medicine. It has historically been used for its antiseptic and analgesic effects. It includes multiple compound that are linked to anti-inflammatory properties. It helps in preventing constipation, promote regularity of digestive system, strengthening the immune system and prevent blood clotting and much more.

    Nutmeg

    Nutmeg is the spice made by grinding the seed of the fragrant nutmeg (Myristica fragrans) tree into powder. The spice has a distinctive pungent fragrance and a warm slightly sweet taste.  Indonesia is the main producer of nutmeg and mace.

    Mace

    Mace is the spice made from the reddish seed covering of the nutmeg seed. Its flavour is similar to nutmeg but more delicate; it is used to flavour baked goods, meat, fish, vegetables and in preserving and pickling

    Dry Ginger

    Dried Ginger is a highly versatile spice used in various ways. Fresh ginger which has undergone a drying process by first washing and soaking overnight and then drying it out in the sun. The most pungent form, however, is dried ginger whose active component is shogaol. Health benefits includes Weight loss, Lowers cholesterol, indigestion, Menstural pain, Nausea, morning sickness and lowers blood sugar.
